How It Works

Three steps. Under a minute.

  1. 1

    Describe your symptoms

    Type what you're feeling — onset, location, severity, and any relevant history. The more detail, the sharper the assessment.

  2. 2

    Aegis asks follow-up questions

    If anything is ambiguous, Aegis will ask targeted clarifying questions before forming its assessment — just like a triage nurse would.

  3. 3

    Receive a structured triage report

    You'll get an urgency level, most likely conditions explained in plain language, and clear guidance on what to do next.
